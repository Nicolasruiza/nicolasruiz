Advisor panel
beyond the prototypes

Not everything is an app. Some of the most useful work is in how the AI itself is set up.

Advisory boards
Panels of experts as standing projects

Persistent projects that convene a synthesized board of named specialists — a health advisory board spanning sleep, metabolic, and hormonal medicine, and a seven-advisor financial board grounded in Canadian tax rules. Each answers with evidence weighed across the pool and per-paragraph attribution, against my real baselines.

Daily operations
AI co-working the day

Scheduled agent sessions that organize the day: reading and triaging email, cleaning up the inbox, and preparing what needs attention — running as recurring tasks rather than one-off prompts.

Integrations
MCPs & connected tools

Model Context Protocol connectors that give the assistant direct access to calendars, documents, and data sources — so analysis and automation work on live information instead of pasted snippets.
